Biography

Zimu Zhou is currently a tenure-track assistant professor at the School of Information Systems (SIS), Singapore Management University (SMU). He received his Ph.D. in 2015 in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), under supervision of Prof. Linoel M. Ni and Prof. Yunhao Liu. He received his B.E. in 2011 in the Department of Electronic Engineering, Tsinghua University. From 2016 to 2019, he was a postdoctoral researcher at the Computer Engineering Group (TEC), ETH Zurich, under supervision of Prof. Dr. Lothar Thiele.

Research Interests

My research focuses on mobile and ubiquitous computing. The vision is to integrate sensing, computing and decision-making into everyday IoT infrastructures for intelligent individual and urban services. My recent research topics include:

  • On-device Machine Learning

  • Federated Spatial Data Services

  • AI-empowered Ubiquitous Applications
